Chapter 01 · The First Day of NEET

The Day We Met In Person

You came right in the middle of the Hello movie and you were NOT letting me take pictures 😤 I had literally skipped college to watch Nuvvu Naku Nachav or Nuvve Nuvve on TV since mom and dad were away. And then you walked in. Best distraction of my entire life. 🫠

📍 The beginning of everything

Chapter 02

The Secret Selfie at Vijaya Akka Wedding

The most tensed selfie ever taken 😅 Your babbai came and SAW us and my heart nearly stopped!! I wore that black shirt you always wanted to see me in — bought it for 2000 rupees from Spykar at Tipsy Topsy. All for you. Every single rupee worth it. 🖤

🖤 You asked for black. I delivered.

Chapter 03

First Time at My Home 🥺

I brought you straight to my home that day when no one else was there. Look how cute you are 🥺 And there I was looking like a complete rowdy 😮‍💨 Still one of my all-time favourite memories. Everything felt different after that day.

🏠 Just us. Nobody else.

Chapter 04

The Famous 5-Day Escape 😋

You said 5 days coaching and came straight to me 😂 B.Tech done, no idea how to meet, but we figured it out. Temple in Ponnuru, eating outside together, just laughing and living like the happiest couple in the world. 5 days that felt like a whole lifetime. 😋

🌊 5 days. A lifetime of memories.

Chapter 05

The Airport Goodbye 😭

I was leaving to UK. You came to see me off. As you walked away, I was crying. You were crying. Then mavayya came and I started recording... and I just KNEW you would turn back. And you did. 🎬 That single moment? Pure cinema. It lives in my heart forever and always.

✈️ You turned back. I will never forget.

Chapter 06

UK → India → USA. Always Together.

Every trip back to India was for you. Every stolen moment between flights, visa runs, and video calls was worth it. Distance tried its absolute hardest. We tried harder. No ocean was ever big enough to keep us apart. 💪🌏

🌍 Oceans crossed. Love never lost.

Chapter 07

The Long Hair Visit 🤫

I had grown my hair long and you were absolutely obsessed — you badly wanted to see it and touch it in person before I cut it. So the moment I got the chance, I went straight to you. No detours. Took a selfie, cropped you out, made it my WhatsApp DP. Even today it is still my DP. 🤫 Nobody knows you are right there beside me in your cute pink frock.

💗 Still my DP. Still my favourite.

Chapter 08 · The Greatest Chapter

Finally. We Got Married. 💍

Both families said yes. The universe said yes. We always said yes. 💍 From being relatives as kids, to me teasing you nonstop, to mardhal, to 8 beautiful years — to THIS. I would do it all again. Every single bit of it. Only for you. Thank you for coming into my life, Bujjamma. You are my everything. 🥹💗

👑 Mrs. Bobby. Officially. Always. Forever.

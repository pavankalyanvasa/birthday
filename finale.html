A letter from Bobby

To my Devika, on her birthday 🌸

💗

8 years ago, a quiet little girl walked into my life as a relative — let me tease her endlessly as a kid, stayed silent through all of it, and somehow, somehow, never left. 🥺

I teased you as a kid. I fell for you as a boy. I missed you as a man. I cried for you at airports. I crossed oceans for you. I waited, and waited, and waited — because nothing worth having ever comes easy.

You are the most beautiful, most quietly stubborn, most fiercely strong person I have ever known. You never made noise about how much you held. You just held it. And you held me too, from across every distance we ever had. 💪

I still think about that airport moment when you turned back. I knew you would. I knew it in my bones. That one second told me everything I ever needed to know about us. 🎬

And now you are Mrs. Bobby. Officially. Finally. The journey from mardhal to married — 8 years, endless moments, two families, and one crazy love story — I would do every single bit of it again without a second thought.

I am sitting here in the USA right now, counting the days until you are beside me. This house, this life, this everything — it is all waiting for you. Come soon. Please. 🛫

Happy Birthday, my love. My mardhal. My best friend. My wife. Today and every day after. 🌸💍

💍

Yours always and forever,
Muddula Mogudu 💗
Mr. Pavan Kalyan
